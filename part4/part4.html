Instructions

Rank the peaks of a graph.

  • Look at the graph and rank the peaks.
  • Start from the major (most obvious) peaks to the minor peaks.
  • Answers that don't contain all the peaks will be rejected (make sure you rank all the peaks).
  • Set the peaks apart by commas.
