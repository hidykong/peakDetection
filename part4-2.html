Instructions

Classify the peaks of a graph.

  • Look at the graph and classify the peaks into three groups.
  • Divide into major (most obvious) peaks, minor peaks (less obvious), and points that are not peaks.
  • Answers that don't contain all the peaks will be rejected (make sure you classify all the peaks).
  • Set the peaks apart by commas.
